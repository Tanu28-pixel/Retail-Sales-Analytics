Category-wise Insights
Technology category recorded the highest sales performance among all categories.
Furniture category generated moderate sales but comparatively lower profit contribution.
Office Supplies maintained balanced sales and profit performance.

Region-wise Insights
West region emerged as the top-performing region in terms of sales.
East region showed strong sales contribution and remained close to the West region.
South region generated the lowest sales among all regions.
Central region maintained steady but moderate sales performance.

Monthly Sales Insights
Sales increased significantly during the final months of the year.
November and December showed peak sales trends, indicating seasonal demand growth.
Early-year months experienced comparatively lower sales activity.
Monthly sales trend reflects consistent business growth over time.

Profit Insights
Technology contributed the highest profit share.
Office Supplies generated stable profit margins.
Furniture category showed relatively lower profitability despite decent sales.
Profit distribution indicates that sales volume and profitability are not always directly proportional.

KPI Insights
Total Sales exceeded 2.30M, indicating strong overall business performance.
Total Profit reached approximately 286K.
Total Orders crossed 9.9K+, showing high customer transaction activity.
Average Sales value remained around 229 per order.

Business Insights
Technology products are driving both revenue and profitability.
West region should be considered the primary target market for future business expansion.
Seasonal demand patterns suggest strong year-end customer purchasing behavior.
Lower-performing regions and categories may require targeted marketing and pricing strategies.
