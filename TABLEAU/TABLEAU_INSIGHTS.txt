1. Sales Trend Analysis
Sales showed fluctuations throughout the year.
Highest sales were recorded during the last quarter, especially in November and December.
February had the lowest sales performance.
Overall sales trend indicates growth towards year-end.

2. Regional Sales Performance
West region generated the highest sales among all regions.
East region was the second best-performing region.
South region contributed the least sales.
Sales distribution varies significantly across regions.

3. Category Contribution
Technology category contributed the highest share of total sales.
Furniture and Office Supplies also contributed significantly.
Category sales contribution appears relatively balanced.

4. Profit Contribution Analysis
Technology category generated the highest profit.
Office Supplies maintained moderate profit contribution.
Furniture contributed comparatively lower profit margins.
Profitability differs across product categories.

5. Business Insights
End-of-year sales growth indicates seasonal demand increase.
Technology products are both high revenue and high profit generating.
South region requires strategic improvement for better sales performance.
Focusing on profitable categories can improve overall business growth.
